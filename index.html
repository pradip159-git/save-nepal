How It Works

Three simple steps to get your issue addressed

📍

1. Select Your Location

Choose your Province, District, Ward and Tole from Nepal's full administrative hierarchy.

📝

2. Describe the Issue

Explain the problem clearly. Attach photos, audio recordings, or documents as evidence.

✅

3. Track Resolution

Receive a unique Complaint ID. Track your issue status from Pending to Resolved.
